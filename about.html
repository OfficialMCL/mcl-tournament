About Us

Meme Coin League (MCL) is a community-driven tournament platform where memecoins battle in weekly elimination rounds. Our mission is to promote engagement, visibility, and social interaction around the funniest and most viral crypto projects.

Founded by a team of blockchain enthusiasts, MCL leverages transparency and decentralized voting mechanisms to ensure fair play and maximum excitement.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I participate in tournaments?

Connect your wallet, buy $MCL tokens, and stake them to vote for your favorite memecoins in active tournaments.

What is $MCL token?

$MCL is our native utility token used for voting, staking, and governance in the MemeTournament ecosystem.

How often are tournaments held?

We host weekly tournaments featuring different memecoin matchups and special events.

What wallets are supported?

We support Phantom, Solflare, Solana Wallet, and MetaMask for connecting to our platform.

How are tournament winners determined?

Winners are determined by the total amount of $MCL tokens staked for each memecoin during the voting period.
